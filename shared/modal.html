Why Track Your Bets?

Meticulous bet tracking is the foundation of successful sports betting. By recording every wager, you gain valuable insights that can transform your betting strategy.

Key Benefits of Bet Tracking:

  • Performance Analysis: Identify which sports, bet types, and strategies work best for you
  • Bankroll Management: Monitor your betting patterns and avoid emotional decisions
  • Risk Assessment: Understand your true win rate and expected value
  • Strategy Refinement: Learn from losses and optimize your approach

What to Track:

  • Bet Details: Date, sport, event, bet type, odds, and stake
  • Outcomes: Wins, losses, pushes, and pending bets
  • Performance Metrics: Win rate, ROI, profit/loss, and bankroll growth
  • Notes: Context about your decision-making process

Best Practices:

  • Track every bet, no matter how small
  • Be honest about outcomes and don't chase losses
  • Review your data regularly to spot trends
  • Set realistic goals and stick to your bankroll limits
